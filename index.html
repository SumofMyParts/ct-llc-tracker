Consequences of Missing the Deadline
Immediately: Loss of good standing — affects financing, contracts, and the ability to properly dissolve.
1+ year delinquent: Secretary of State sends an administrative dissolution notice; 3 months to cure by filing.
Dissolution: LLC loses all legal authority — cannot enter contracts, sue, or be sued in its name. May lose the business name.
Reinstatement: $120 reinstatement fee + back filing fees + proof of paid taxes required.

CT Pass-Through Entity Tax (CT-PET) — Optional Election
Who: Multi-member LLCs and S-Corps may elect to pay CT-PET at the entity level.
Benefit: Members can claim a credit on personal CT income tax — a workaround to the federal SALT deduction cap.
Election: Annual and irrevocable once made for the year. Must be elected each tax year.
Payment due: March 15 (calendar-year entities).
Extended filing: September 15 — but payment remains due March 15.
